About Airport Al Hoceima

The Airport Al Hoceima is an international airport located in the city of Al Hoceima in Morocco. The airport serves both domestic and international flights.It is the second-busiest airport in the Tanger-Tetouan-Al Hoceima region of northern Morocco. The airport is named after the 12th century CE Moroccan geographer and cartographer Al-Idrisi.

Facilities

The airport resides at an elevation of 95 feet (29 m) above mean sea level. It has one runway designated 17/35 with an asphalt surface measuring 2,500 by 45 metres
